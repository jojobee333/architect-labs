# Architecture Scorecard

Score 1-5 for each category.

## Modularity
Are boundaries explicit and enforceable?

## Simplicity
Is the design as simple as possible for the requirements?

## Testability
Can behavior be validated at unit, integration, and contract levels?

## Operability
Are logging, metrics, tracing, rollout, and rollback addressed?

## Scalability
Does the design scale where it needs to, and only there?

## Security
Are trust boundaries, secrets, authz, and auditability handled?

## Changeability
Can major requirements change without repo-wide surgery?

## Tradeoff Clarity
Are the reasons for this design documented in ADRs?
